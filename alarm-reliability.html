Alarm Reliability Guide

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Discipline100 uses iPhone notifications and alarm sound to help wake you up. For best reliability, check the settings below before relying on an alarm.

Before your alarm:
  • Keep notifications on
  • Allow Discipline100 in Focus/Sleep Mode
  • Leave Discipline100 open in the background

Allow Discipline100 in Focus/Sleep Mode

If Focus, Do Not Disturb, or Sleep Mode is active, iPhone may silence app notifications unless Discipline100 is allowed.

Option 1: Allow the app in your Focus

  1. Open the iPhone Settings app.
  2. Tap Focus.
  3. Choose the Focus you use at night, such as Sleep or Do Not Disturb.
  4. Tap Apps under allowed notifications.
  5. Add Discipline100.

Option 2: Check Sleep Schedule

  1. Open the Health app.
  2. Tap Browse, then Sleep.
  3. Review your sleep schedule and Focus settings.
  4. Make sure Discipline100 is allowed if Sleep Focus turns on overnight.

Keep Notifications On

  1. Open iPhone Settings.
  2. Tap Notifications.
  3. Select Discipline100.
  4. Turn on Allow Notifications.
  5. Make sure sound alerts are enabled.

Leave Discipline100 Open in the Background

After setting your alarm, return to the Home Screen or lock your phone. Avoid swiping Discipline100 away from the app switcher before your alarm.

Test Your Alarm Sound

Use the sound test in Discipline100 after setting an alarm. If you do not hear the test, check your volume, speaker, Bluetooth audio, and notification settings.

Common Reasons an Alarm May Not Sound

  • Notifications are disabled for Discipline100.
  • Focus, Do Not Disturb, or Sleep Mode is blocking notifications.
  • The app was closed from the app switcher.
  • The phone volume is too low.
  • Audio is connected to headphones or another Bluetooth device.
  • The phone is in Low Power Mode or under heavy system restrictions.

iOS controls some alarm and background behavior. Discipline100 cannot guarantee alarm sound in every device state, so these settings help reduce the chance of a missed alarm.
