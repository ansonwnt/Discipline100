Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Discipline100 ("the App") is developed and maintained by Discipline100. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

All data — including your alarm settings, snooze history, score, and name — is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on your device using local storage. If you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted. We cannot recover it.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Alarm/Notification permission: Required to fire alarms at your scheduled time, even when the app is closed.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback when interacting with the app.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
