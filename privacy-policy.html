Privacy, Terms & Refund Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Discipline100 ("the App") is developed and maintained by Discipline100. This page explains how we handle your information, the basic terms for using the App, and how deposits and withdrawals work.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect or process account information such as your email address or sign-in provider, your display name, alarm settings, snooze history, deposit tier, balance, and payment-related transaction records.

Payment card details are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number or card security code.

Data Storage

Some App data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, certain data may also be synced with Firebase services so your account, balance, alarms, and history can work across sessions and devices.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including Firebase for authentication, cloud data storage, and cloud functions; Stripe for payment processing and refunds; Apple Sign In; and Google Sign-In. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Terms of Service

By using the App, you agree to use it only for lawful purposes and to provide accurate account and payment information. You are responsible for setting alarms correctly, keeping notifications enabled, and not force-quitting the App if you want alarms to fire reliably.

The App is intended as a motivational alarm and habit tool. We do not guarantee that alarms, notifications, or background behavior will work in every circumstance, because device settings, operating system restrictions, network conditions, and user actions can affect reliability.

Deposits, Snooze Charges, and Refunds

Deposits are refundable balances, not purchases. Snooze charges are deducted from your deposit according to the tier you choose in the App. Your first snooze for each alarm is free. After that, paid snoozes reduce your remaining balance.

You may request withdrawal of your remaining balance from inside the App. Refunds are returned through Stripe to the original payment method when possible and may take several business days to appear, depending on your bank or card issuer. Amounts already deducted for paid snoozes are not refunded.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Alarm/Notification permission: Required to fire alarms at your scheduled time, even when the app is closed.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback when interacting with the app.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
