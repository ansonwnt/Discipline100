Frequently Asked Questions

My alarm didn't go off

Alarm reliability depends on your device settings. Make sure:

  • Notifications are enabled for Discipline100 in iOS Settings
  • The app is allowed in your Focus or Sleep mode
  • Your phone volume is turned up
  • You have not force-quit the app

See our full Alarm Reliability Guide for step-by-step instructions.

How do deposits work?

Your deposit is a refundable balance, not a purchase. When you snooze your alarm beyond your free snooze, a small amount is deducted from your deposit as a penalty. Your remaining balance can be withdrawn at any time from inside the app.

How do I withdraw my balance?

Go to the Balance tab inside the app and tap Withdraw. Refunds are processed through Stripe and typically appear within 3–5 business days depending on your bank.

I was charged for a snooze but I didn't snooze

Contact us at admin@eat-100.com with your account email and the date of the charge and we will investigate and refund if applicable.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at admin@eat-100.com with your account email and we will delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.
